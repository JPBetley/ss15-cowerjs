About Chips Or Something

Chips Or Something is a web app to help you collaboratively plan the food and drink for your next big event. Import your events from Facebook, invite your guests, and decide on a list of what to bring. Then, you and your guests can decide who's bringing what, then post the final list back to Facebook for posterity!

Here's an example: John has made plans to have Joe, James, Jake and Jane over to watch the Big Game next weekend. But they're going to need something to munch on during all those timeouts and commercial breaks. So John logs into Chips Or Something and imports his Facebook event, inviting his friends to join him on COS. There, they agree that Pizza, Wings, Beer, Salad and (of course!) Chips are necessary items to bring. Each person claims an item they are willing to bring. John uses COS to make a pinned post back on the Facebook Event page with the now-completed list. The following Sunday, everyone brings their A-game, and also their item!

FAQ

I'm hosting an event. How can I use Chips Or Something?

Log in, and you'll be directed to the My Events page. Click the Import Event button, and choose the Facebook event that you'd like to import. COS will give you a link and an option to post that link to your Facebook Event page. That's the invite link your guests will use.

If you've already imported your event and invited your guests, simply select the event from My Events and you'll be directed to the View Events page, where you can participate in the planning process! Once planning is done, be sure to click the Finalize List button to post the list of who's bringing what to your Facebook Events page.

I got invited to an event using Chips Or Something. What do I do?

Just log in, and you'll be directed to the View Event page. From there, simply type in items you want to suggest, vote Yay or Nay on Suggested Items, and claim any Accepted Items you want to bring. If you want a reminder, simply look at your Facebook's event page.

I don't have a Facebook. Can I still use Chips Or Something?

Sorry, no. COS is meant to be used with Facebook Events. However, we may expand to support Google Calendar events in the future, so stay tuned!
